OUR CONCEPT

We have developed an express beauty salon particularly suited to areas of strong flows such as airports.

All women would like to fly off beautifully the day they leave. Now no need to make an appointment with different providers before your trip. At Check-in beauty you can make your polish poses, your makeup and your hair while waiting for your flight.

 

Be on time for your beauty boarding!
